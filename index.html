Our Story

Born from a Passion for Desserts

Heaven in a Bite started as a dream — to bring premium café-style cheesecakes and cake bowls to everyone. What began in a small kitchen in Panvel has grown into a beloved dessert brand.

Every Bowl we create is a labor of love: hand-layered with the finest cream cheese, real chocolate, and seasonal fruits. We believe that luxury desserts should be accessible, personal, and absolutely unforgettable.
